Our Mission, Vision, & Goal

Vision

A diocese that embraces Jesus' Spirituality through renewed Social apostolate that includes Prophetic involvement in politics in communion with the Materially poor as empowered Agents of social transformation.

Mission

Form social consciousness and Social conscience that works for justice and a new social order.

Goal

To form and strengthen Parish Social Action committee in all Parishes and chaplaincies to render effective social pastoral services for the poor.

Our Activities

Health Program

We run a community-based health program, primarily for mothers and single parents, offering training and establishing health centers in local parishes.

Scholarship Program

A four-year college scholarship that provides students with monthly allowances for transportation and other miscellaneous needs.

Humanitarian & Charitable Works

We organize humanitarian responses through relief services, including a weekly Friday distribution of rice and feeding programs for the needy.
